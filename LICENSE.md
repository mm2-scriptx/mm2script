- ❌ **GUI Does Not Appear After Execution**
  The **Mm2 Script Gui** sometimes fails to render if the executor injects before the game has fully loaded. **Solution:** Wait until you see your character in the lobby before pressing **Execute**, then try again.

- ❌ **Silent Aim Not Connecting Shots**
  The **Murder Mystery 2 Script Silent Aim** module requires a level-6 executor or higher. **Solution:** Verify your executor tier and increase `SilentAimFOV` to `120` in the config as a test.

- ❌ **Auto Farm Stops After Disconnection**
  If the **Mm2 Script Auto Farm Candy** loop halts after a network drop, your version may be outdated. **Solution:** Update to `v4.9.2` or later, which includes server-hop persistence for the farm module.

- ❌ **Item Spawner Returns Empty Inventory**
  The **Item Spawner Mm2 Script** can fail if the game's asset servers are slow. **Solution:** Retry after switching to a less populated server, or set `ItemPool` to `"permanent"` in the config.

- ❌ **Hitbox Multiplier Has No Effect**
  Some lower-tier executors do not support the render hooks needed for the **Murder Mystery 2 Script Hitbox** module. **Solution:** Upgrade to a level-6+ executor or reduce `HitboxMultiplier` to `2.0`.

- ❌ **Executor Crashes on Inject**
  This is rarely caused by the **Mm2 Script** itself. **Solution:** Clear your executor's cache folder at `C:/Desktop/YourExecutor/cache/`, restart, and inject again.
